My Personal Interests

I especially like philosophy and history and sometimes I read random articles or watch videos about these topics.

I like to play the piano and cello. I have been playing the piano for about 12 years and the cello for about 7.5 years. I mostly like to play songs from composers like Bach, Beethoven, Debussy, Chopin, etc. This is why I like the musical group The Piano Guys, as they consist of two guys who play the piano and cello. I like listening to soundtracks or songs and trying to play them on the piano and cello. Other things I like to do include playing board games with my family, playing chess, and sometimes video games. Some board games I like include Risk, Catan, and Monopoly. I also like programming and have made a few simple games with Python plus some other projects involving Javascript.
